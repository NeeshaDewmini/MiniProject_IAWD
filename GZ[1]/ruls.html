Rules & Guidelines For Playing

1. Only Game Zone club members will be allowed to play.


2. Payment of entrance fees/Subcriptions/Sectional Fees


The entrance fee shall be paid in full at the time of joinning the club.

All Subscription & Sectional fees should be paid in full before the 31" of Jan each year


3. Members are liable for any damage casued to the Club property by them or their guestsPlaying Members Shall be persons who are invited to represent the Club in Cricket/Tennis/Swimming and any other Sport and are endorsed as such by the respective House Committee and approved by the Executive Committee..


4. Applications for traning must compulsorily attach a medical fitness certificate to the membership application form.


5. If you are unable to attend traning for a period exceeding one month, you inform the sports club.A Member is considered an absentee member if he/ she has duly notified the Secretary of the Club his/ her impending absence for a period over 12 months from Sri Lanka in conformity with Section 11


6. Any resolution or amendment submitted & reject by the membership shall not be re-submitted for one year.
