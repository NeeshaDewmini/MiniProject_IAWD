About of our Chess


Chess is a strategic and intellectually stimulating game that sharpens your mind and enhances critical thinking skills. At our club, we offer comprehensive training programs to help you understand the game's intricacies and develop effective strategies

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our club provides the resources and expert coaching to improve your skills and deepen your appreciation for chess.

About our Practices


Join us to not only play chess but also to learn tactics, develop your problem-solving abilities, and cultivate discipline, all in a supportive and professional environment. We help individuals grow in both skills and confidence, fostering a lifelong passion for the game.

Our club features comfortable and conducive spaces for practice, and we organize regular tournaments to test your skills and enhance your competitive spirit. Join us to learn, practice, and excel in chess
